High Chicago Public Schools
posted on Wednesday, February 1, 2017

ATTENTION SUBSTITUTES

Starting immediately, please adopt the following new procedure for  Kronos clock usage whenever you work as a substitute at Chicago Public Schools:

At the start of your shift:  

Press "substitute" button on the Kronos clock

Swipe your card or enter your employee ID into the Kronos clock

At the end of your shift:

Do not press the "substitute" button on the Kronos clock

Swipe your card or enter your employee ID number into the Kronos clock.
